    About Me

    Who Am I

    Introduction

    My name is Ng Kha Meng. I’m a graphic designer and front-end developer. At the age of 14, I started on the path of design. I’ve always been fascinated by the world of art: the harmony; the contrasts; the hidden messages.

    Fast forward to this day, I’ve learned and grown a lot. I’ve gained experience and created things that I couldn’t have imagined before. There were some obstacles along the way, but I wasn’t discouraged. It was truly a journey of a lifetime.

    Philosophy

    I truly believe that designers are incredibly passionate in their craft. I am too. My conduct is to help you, the client, as best as I can. I will not always do what you request, but rather what is best for you and your business. Rest assured, I will assist you to achieve your goals, and leave a lasting impression on your business.

    I work by the ideology that the purpose of design is to make people’s lives better. Designs that are aesthetically pleasing, yet doesn’t solve a problem are absolutely useless. I design to serve a purpose, and that purpose is to leave this world a better place than I entered it.

    Services

    I’ve worked on a diverse set of projects with a lot of talented people. This experience has enabled me hone and expand my craft. Thus, I offer a variety of services: branding and identity, print design, clothing design, website and app design and website creation.

    Even if your project isn’t listed here, contact me and we can work something out.

    Availability

    I’m available for hire as a freelancer and able to work on various projects on a contract basis.
